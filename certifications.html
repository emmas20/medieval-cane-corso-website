Licences, Registrations & Health Certifications

Breeding licences, registrations, health-testing results, and veterinary certifications for our program, all in one place. Microchip numbers, application numbers, and other sensitive details are kept private and are not published here.

What This Means

Why Health Testing Matters

OFA (Orthopedic Foundation for Animals) evaluations are a widely recognized, independent way to screen breeding dogs for hip and elbow dysplasia and cardiac issues before they are bred. A result documented here reflects the finding of that specific evaluation on the date shown — it is one part of a responsible breeding program, alongside structure, temperament, and pedigree.
